About Us

Welcome to the NGO Management System (NMS), dedicated to providing free school education for underprivileged children. Our system efficiently manages student assistance, funds, donor engagement, resource inventory, and financial accountability. Student Assistance We maintain student records and provide essential support like books, uniforms, and financial aid. Fund Management We estimate and allocate funds based on student needs, ensuring effective resource utilization. Donor Engagement Our donors contribute regularly, helping us sustain our operations and support more children. Resource Inventory We manage donations of books, uniforms, and other items to meet student needs each year. Financial Accountability We maintain transparent records of expenses, ensuring every rupee is spent wisely. Join us in our mission to transform lives through education. Together, we can build a brighter future for all.
